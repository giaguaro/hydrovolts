HydroVolts Solar Leaf™

From alpine campsites to Arctic hamlets, HydroVolts unlocks 24 / 7 renewable energy—proudly engineered and built in Canada.

How It Works

Step Headline What Happens
1 Harvest the Sun Biomimetic “leaves” capture daylight and split water—no fragile glass to shatter, no moving parts to seize in snow.
2 Store Safely Solid‑state hydride beads lock hydrogen at low pressure; earth‑abundant catalysts keep costs low and supply chains local.
3 Power Anytime Fuel‑cell stack delivers silent, emission‑free electricity after dark, through storms, or across long Canadian winters.

Who We Power

Campers & Overlanders

Charge lanterns, GPS units, e‑bikes or induction cooktops without lugging propane canisters or noisy generators.

Off‑Grid & Indigenous Communities

Supplement diesel with a modular, serviceable system that thrives in −40 °C blizzards, reducing fuel costs and emissions while creating local maintenance jobs.

Homeless & Urban Resilience Programs

Deploy compact “Solar Leaf™ shelters” that supply lighting, phone charging and Wi‑Fi hubs—improving safety and connectivity for vulnerable populations.

Disaster‑Relief & Wildfire Crews

Fly‑in/drive‑in kits provide continuous power for radios, medical fridges and water‑purification pumps when the grid is down.

Why Canadian‑Made Matters

  • Designed for Harsh Climates – Every panel, gasket and control board is tested from coastal rain to prairie ice fog.
  • Local Supply Chains – Nickel‑iron catalysts, aluminum enclosures and hydride alloys are sourced and fabricated domestically, supporting skilled Canadian labour.
  • Regulatory Peace of Mind – Certified to CSA C22.2 standards and Transport Canada guidelines for low‑pressure hydrogen storage—no special permits required for remote transport.
