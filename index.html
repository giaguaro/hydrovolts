How It Works

1

Harvest the Sun

Biomimetic “leaves” capture daylight and split water—no fragile glass to shatter, no moving parts to seize in snow.

2

Store Safely

Solid‑state hydride beads lock hydrogen at low pressure; earth‑abundant catalysts keep costs low and supply chains local.

3

Power Anytime

Fuel‑cell stack delivers silent, emission‑free electricity after dark, through storms, or across long Canadian winters.

Who We Power

Campers & Overlanders

Charge lanterns, GPS units, e‑bikes or induction cooktops without lugging propane canisters or noisy generators.

Off‑Grid & Indigenous Communities

Supplement diesel with a modular system that thrives in −40 °C blizzards, slashing fuel use while creating local jobs.

Homeless & Urban Resilience

Disaster‑Relief & Wildfire Crews
